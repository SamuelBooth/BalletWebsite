About Me And Contact Me

My experience with dance first started in my youth when my family and I dropped off my cousin at a dance class. Unfortunately, this had little impression on me. Fast forward to high school I was asked to participate in my townâ€™s local studio for a spring show. After accepting this spontaneous experience, I found a passion for movement and switched to full time Ballet Classes. After graduating Highschool and then attending a 6-week summer intensive in Orlando on scholarship. I accepted a full training position at BalletMet In Columbus, Ohio. After a wonderful year and more than 30 Professional shows completed with the BalletMet. I transferred to a small Ballet company in Fort Wayne, Indiana. Their I played Core de Ballet, Soloist and Lead roles including Lead Phantom, from Phantom of the Opera and Arabian Coffee Soloist, from The Nutcracker. 4 years later of being a Professional Ballet Dancer I was equipped with a wealth of life changing experiences. I can honestly say Ballet saved my mind, body and spirit. Movement through space is a gift from God. I hope you can find yourself through any form of dance too.
